About me

I am currently a postgraduate student of the School of Management at the University of St. Andrews. I am studying Marketing and am embracing this course passionately. My free time is mainly dedicated to my friends and social activities in St. Andrews including balls but also academic events such as listening tovisiting lecturers. Most recently, I have participated in the Loreal Marketing Challenge where I have been rather successful.

Until I started studying in St. Andrews in September 2015, photography has just been a hobby. I enjoyed sunny days out, taking pictures as well as creating memories while spending time with my friends and family.

However, one can say that I have been ‘discovered’ in St. Andrews. Over the first few months in this little Scottish town, many of my friend and acquaintances have seen my photos and I was always spotted with a camera in my hands. Soon enough, I was asked by the organisers of ‘Don’t Walk’ to be the photographer of the night. This is where my career started.

From this point onwards, I became increasingly known for my skills and hence I was also able develop my skillset. Now I am ready to phptograph you!
